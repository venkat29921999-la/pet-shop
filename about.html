Our Story

From one grooming van to a nationwide family

Pawfetch began when our founder couldn't find a single trustworthy groomer for her rescue dog, Biscuit. So she became one — driving a van across the city, learning what pet parents actually needed.

Today that single van has grown into a full shop and care network, but the mission hasn't changed: treat every pet like Biscuit, and every parent like family.
